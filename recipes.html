Recipes

Breakfast:

  • Overnight Oats: Combine rolled oats, milk (dairy or plant-based), chia seeds, and your favorite fruits and nuts.
  • Greek Yogurt with Berries and Granola: A quick and protein-packed breakfast.
  • Avocado Toast: Whole-wheat toast topped with mashed avocado, a sprinkle of salt and pepper, and a squeeze of lemon juice.

Dinner:

  • Salmon with Roasted Vegetables: Salmon is a great source of omega-3 fatty acids. Serve with colorful roasted veggies for a balanced meal.
  • Chicken Stir-Fry: A quick and easy dinner with plenty of vegetables
  • Lentil Curry: A vegetarian protein-rich meal that's packed with flavor

Snacks:

  • Fruit and Nut Mix: A healthy combination of carbohydrates, fiber, and protein.
  • Hummus with Veggies: Hummus is a great source of protein and fiber.
  • Hard-Boiled Eggs: A simple and protein-rich snack.
